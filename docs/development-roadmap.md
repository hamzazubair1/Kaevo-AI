## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Slow client acquisition | Aggressive outreach, content marketing, partnerships |
| Technical complexity | Start simple, iterate, hire specialists |
| Cash flow constraints | Retainer-first model, pre-payment for projects |
| Team scaling | Document everything, SOPs, remote-first culture |
| Market competition | Focus on niche (SMB AI automation), superior service |
| AI model dependencies | Multi-model support, open-source fallbacks |
