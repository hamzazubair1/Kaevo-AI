## Logo Usage

### Primary Logo
- Use the full "Kaevo AI" logo on light and dark backgrounds
- Maintain minimum clear space equal to the height of the "K" around all sides

### Logo Variations
| Variant | Use Case |
|---------|----------|
| Full Logo (Light) | Light backgrounds |
| Full Logo (Dark) | Dark backgrounds |
| Icon Only | Favicons, app icons, small spaces |
| Horizontal | Headers, navigation bars |

### Logo Don'ts
- ❌ Don't stretch or distort the logo
- ❌ Don't change the logo colors
- ❌ Don't place the logo on busy backgrounds
- ❌ Don't rotate the logo
- ❌ Don't add effects (shadows, gradients, outlines)

## Tone of Voice

### Writing Principles

| Principle | Description | Example |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| **Clear** | Simple language, avoid jargon | "We build AI chatbots" not "We leverage NLP-powered conversational interfaces" |
| **Confident** | Assertive but not arrogant | "Our AI agents deliver results" not "Our AI agents might help" |
| **Approachable** | Friendly and human | "Let's automate your workflow" not "Initiate workflow automation protocol" |
| **Action-Oriented** | Focus on outcomes | "Save 40 hours per week" not "Efficiency improvements available" |

### Do's and Don'ts

| ✅ Do | ❌ Don't |
|-------|---------|
| Speak in active voice | Use passive voice |
| Use "we" and "you" | Use third person |
| Be specific with numbers | Be vague about results |
| Explain AI in simple terms | Use unnecessary technical jargon |
| Focus on business outcomes | Focus only on technical features |

## Imagery Guidelines

### Photography Style
- Modern, clean, and tech-forward
- Diverse representation
- Natural lighting preferred
- Avoid stock photo clichés (handshakes, generic office scenes)

### Illustration Style
- Geometric and clean
- Use brand colors
- Subtle gradients and shadows
- Consistent line weights

### Icon Style
- Outline icons (2px stroke)
- Rounded corners
- Brand color accents
- Lucide Icons or Phosphor Icons preferred

## Social Media Standards

### Profile Picture
- Use the Kaevo AI icon mark
- Ensure readability at small sizes

### Cover Images
- Use brand gradient backgrounds
- Include tagline or key messaging
- Maintain consistent visual style

### Post Templates
- Use brand colors and fonts
- Include the Kaevo AI logo
- Maintain white space
- Use consistent formatting
